Hello! I am Louis Fournier, a 22 year old french student specialized in Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. I am passionnate about anything related to Artificial Intelligence, Music, Graphism...

After high-school in Lille, I follow preparatory classes (Maths-Physics) in Valenciennes at Lycée Henri Wallon and at Versailles at Lycée Sainte Geneviève. I got accepted in Ecole Polytechnique in the Cursus Ingénieur in 2016. For my fourth year, I got accepted into the double-cursus of Télécom Paris as an engineer and the Research Master MVA (Maths, Vision, Learning) of ENS Paris-Saclay. I am currently in an internship at INRIA Paris in the CoML (Cognitive Machine Learning) team, studying the geometry of word embeddings.
